Sacred Telugu Wedding Rituals
పవిత్ర ఆచారాలు
❃
Nischayatartham
నిశ్చయతార్థం
The formal engagement ceremony where both families exchange gifts and set the wedding date
Mandap Pooja
మండప పూజ
Consecration of the wedding canopy with prayers, inviting divine energies to bless the ceremony
Kanyadanam
కన్యాదానం
The sacred gifting of the bride — considered the most meritorious act in Hindu tradition
Talambralu
తలంబ్రాలు
The couple showers each other with rice and turmeric as family members bless them with joy
Saptapadi
సప్తపది
Seven sacred steps around the holy fire, each step a lifelong vow of love, loyalty and companionship
Mangalyadharanam
మంగళ్యధారణం
The groom ties the sacred Mangalsutra around the bride's neck — the holiest moment of the wedding
